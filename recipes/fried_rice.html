Chicken Fried Rice Recipe

Home

Chicken fried rice is a comforting, savory classic that brings together tender, bite-sized pieces of seasoned chicken with perfectly wok-tossed grains of rice. The chicken is often marinated or flash-fried first to lock in its juices, ensuring every mouthful is succulent rather than dry. Mixed with crisp bits of carrots, peas, and green onions, the entire dish is brought together by the rich, umami depth of soy sauce and a hint of sesame oil, making it a deeply satisfying, all-in-one meal.

The true magic of this dish lies in the high-heat cooking process, known as wok hei (the breath of the wok), which infuses the rice grains with a subtle, smoky aroma. Each grain of rice gets beautifully coated in the seasoned oils and savory juices rendered from the chicken, striking a perfect balance between soft, fluffy interiors and slightly crisp edges. It is a hearty, time-tested favorite that delivers a comforting warmth with every single spoonful.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice
  • 1 cup diced chicken
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, green onions)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a wok over high heat.
  2. Push the rice to one side and scramble the eggs in the other side.
  3. Add the chicken and vegetables, stirring constantly for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, stirring to combine.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
